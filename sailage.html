HIGH NUTRITIONAL VALUE

Silage is an excellent source of nutrients for cattle feed. Being processed from corn, NutriRich Silage by Vishwa Agro Tech contains higher levels of metabolizable energy (ME), crude protein (CP) and neutral detergent fiber (NDF) than hay or grain. The fermentation process that takes place during the storage of silage improves the digestibility of the crop, making it more accessible to the cattle. The nutrients in silage are also more concentrated than in fresh forage, which means that cattle can get more nutrients from smaller amounts of feed. This makes silage a cost-effective plant nutrition option for farmers. They are essential for growth, milk production, and overall health. Being the ideal silage for healthy cattle, NutriRich Corn Silage contains a high percentage of fermentable carbohydrates, including sugars and starches.
